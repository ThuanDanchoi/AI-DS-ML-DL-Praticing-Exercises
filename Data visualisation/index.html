Introduction

Welcome to our website where we explore the intricate connections between diet, obesity, and life expectancy through innovative data visualizations. Utilizing dot plots and maps, our visual tools highlight the effects of dietary choices on public health across different countries. Our dot plots compare food consumption with obesity rates, while our maps illustrate the geographic distribution of obesity and its impact on life expectancy. Dive into our processed and curated data to discover how global dietary patterns influence health outcomes, providing valuable insights for policymakers, researchers, and the public interested in nutrition and health.

Welcome to the Interactive Data Map

This interactive map provides a visual representation of various data types across different countries over the years. Explore trends in Fat, Protein, and more to gain insights into global nutritional habits and health metrics.

Use the controls on the left to select continets, different years and data types. The map will update to reflect the information of your choice, providing a dynamic way to analyze data visually.

This tool is designed to aid researchers, students, and anyone interested in global health trends to easily access and interpret complex datasets through a user-friendly interface.

Welcome to the Interactive Scatter Plot

This scatter plot provides insights into the distribution of various metrics across different regions. Use the controls to explore different data views.

Welcome to the Interactive Sankey Diagram

This Sankey diagram visualizes the flow of data between different stages or categories. Use it to trace the connections and measure transfer magnitudes.
